Clash Royale

Clash Royale is a mobile game released by Supercell in the spring of 2016. The game is set in the same world of their popular game "Clash of Clans", and has some recurring characters from the mentioned series. While also adding some new ones to stand out as it's own title in the series. The game quickly rose to the top of the charts for most lucrative game on the app store, most popular game, and most popular app, Supercell saw these numbers, and have consistently released large updates for the game, some just balance changes, and some complete overhauls to how the game is played. This has been going on consistently for the past 6 years, and shows no signs of stopping anytime soon.
